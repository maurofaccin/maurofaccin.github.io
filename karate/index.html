Shotokan Karate

Since I was very young I've been taining in karate. I always managed to follow shotokan, one of the most traditional styles of karate. I trained since I was 9 and I'm actually II Dan. In this long period I stepped in a number of dojos and associations in different cities and countries:

  • Shin dojo (Costabissara): my first dojo with M. Paolo Scapin (FIKTA (ITKF), FESIK (WUKO)). In this context I participate to several regional competitions and some national (I was several time regional champion in kumite and once reached a third place in kumite in a national competition).
  • Almozara Sport Club (Zaragoza, Spain)
  • DimensioneK, with M. Roberto Napoli (FIKTA (ITKF)) (Turin, Italy)
  • Deai M. De Troch Claude (JKA) (Ottignies, Belgium)
