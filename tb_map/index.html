TB Maps

Cartography of predicted tuberculosis in high burden settings

Lang: [fr]

This is a work in progress.

Incidence refers to the values estimated by our approach.

Thanks to a research fund granted by the Brussels Region in Belgium (Innoviris), a consortium of Universities (UCLouvain and KULeuven) and a start-up active in the development of innovative tools for international health (Savics) are developing innovative solutions to help:

  • to target very precisely the geographical areas throughout the world with a very high risk of tuberculosis;
  • to facilitate the operational response to address them.

This site is dedicated to the first chapter, see here for more informations.

We invite you to visit Savics Mediscout website for more information on the operational tools developed.
