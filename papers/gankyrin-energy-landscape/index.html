Mapping the Topography of a Protein Energy Landscape

2015-11-19

A new paper from my old PhD work is out. In collaboration with the Itzhaki experimental lab group, we applied our simple model for protein folding (based on WMSE) to the Gankyrin protein. With this approach, both experimental and theoretical, we were able to unveil non trivial folding/unfolding paths.
